# 🎬 THE AGENCY — CEO Cheat Sheet for Film & TV Studios

> Your AI agent army, mapped to every critical function of a modern film & television studio.

---

## 🧭 What Is "The Agency"?

**The Agency** is a modular AI agent system. Each agent is a specialized AI personality with a defined role, workflow, and deliverable format. You deploy them individually or orchestrate them in pipelines. As CEO, you just need to know **which agent to call for which problem.**

---

## ⚡ Power Agents: Your Starting Point

| Agent | What It Does For You |
|---|---|
| 🎬 **Studio Producer** | Your strategic COO-in-an-agent. Manages your entire project portfolio, allocates resources, tracks ROI (targets 25% return), and produces board-ready strategic reviews. **Start here.** |
| 🎭 **Studio Operations** | Day-to-day efficiency — process optimization, scheduling, crew resource coordination |
| 🎯 **Agents Orchestrator** | Chains multiple agents together into automated pipelines (e.g., script → marketing → distribution) |
